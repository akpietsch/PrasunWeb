prn Parun, Paruni, Prasun, Vasi vari, Veron, Verou, Veruni, Wasi-Veri 8,000 (2011), increasing. 1,600 monolinguals. Ethnic population: 8,000. Nurestan province: Sech, Shupu (Ishtivi, Shtevgrom), Ucu, Ushut, and Zumu villages; Prasun (Parun) valley on upper reaches of Pech river; small border area with Badakhshan province. Afghanistan 6a (Vigorous). Indo-European, Indo-Iranian, Nuristani Upper Wasi-Weri (Ishtivi), Central Prasun, Lower Prasun (Pashki, Ushut). Related to Kati [bsh] but more archaic. The most aberrant Nuristani language. No mutual intelligibility of other Nuristani languages. All domains. All ages. Positive attitudes. Also use Dari [prs], Southern Pashto [pbt]. Literacy rate in L2: 15%. OLAC resources in and about Prasuni Unwritten [Qaax].
